Research Objectives

The purpose of this study is to examine the key factors that influence job satisfaction among employees working in small and medium enterprises, with particular attention to both intrinsic factors, such as motivation and recognition, and extrinsic factors, such as salary, workload, and working conditions. The study seeks to assess the overall level of job satisfaction within SMEs and to understand how different workplace conditions and organizational practices shape employeesâ€™ attitudes toward their jobs
