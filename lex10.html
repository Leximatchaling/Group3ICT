Research objectives

The goal of this study is to understand how saluyot leaf extract affects the gut microbiota in rats to see if it can improve digestive health. The gut microbiota is a community of microbes essential for digestive health, and an imbalance can lead to digestive issues. Saluyot is a healthy vegetable rich in compounds that may act as prebiotics, food for good bacteria. While saluyot is known for its health benefits. This research will determine if saluyot extract can increase the diversity and number of beneficial bacteria and if these changes lead to improved digestive health. The findings will provide scientific evidence to support saluyot as a natural option for enhancing gut health.
