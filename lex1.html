Research objectives

This study investigates the UV-protective properties of Saluyot leaf extract for use in eco-friendly sunscreen. This research will examine how different amounts of Saluyot leaf extract can improve a sunscreen’s base ability to block UV light. The method includes preparing Saluyot leaf extract, adding a controlled amount of sunscreen base and then visually testing their UV blocking ability using UV-sensitive films. The goal of this research is to find the best amount of Saluyot extract that offers the most UV protection. This study aims to show Saluyot’s potential as a natural ingredient for safer and more environmentally friendly sunscreens, offering a plant-based solution for sun protection, an alternative to man-made chemical sunscreens.
