Statement of the Problem

1. What is the level of E. coli surface contamination in tuba vinegar before treatment with garlic and acetic acid?
2. Does the structure of garlic bulbs (intact, sliced, or crushed) significantly affect the reduction of E. coli in tuba vinegar?
3. How does the antibacterial effect of garlic alone compare to acetic acid alone in reducing E. coli contamination?
