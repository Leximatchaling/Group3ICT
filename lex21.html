Statement of the Problem

1. How accurately can the AURA-BEACON detect and interpret different LED hazard signals under varying lighting conditions?
2. What is the response time of the AURA-BEACON in translating LED hazard signals into audio alerts during simulated calamities?
3. How clear and effective are the audio alerts produced by the AURA-BEACON in ensuring accessibility for visually impaired individuals and vulnerable populations?
4. To what extent does the integration of audio alerts alongside LED signals improve community responsiveness and situational awareness during emergencies compared to LED-only systems?
