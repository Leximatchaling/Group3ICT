Statement of the Problem

1. How can low-cost image analysis tools be used to detect and quantify Xanthomonas campestris infection in okra plants?
2. What is the spatial distribution of Xanthomonas campestris infection across the okra plot during the observation period?
3. How does the severity of infection change over time as observed through periodic image collection?
4. What patterns of disease spread (hotspots, progression direction, or clustering) can be identified using spatiotemporal mapping?
